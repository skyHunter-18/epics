Our Aim

Waste management and disposal has widely become a major issue in India. The municipal bodies in most districts even with their best efforts sometimes is not able to clean up and manage all the garbage in various areas of a city.
Our project , titled - "Nagar seva" is an android application is our attempt to solve this problem.

This application will provide a transparent and open complaint-redressal solution for the residents of a community and the nagar-nigam or municipal corporation of their localities. By using the app, residents of a particular area will be able to report garbage that has been neglected in their locality to the municipal corporation, so that the garbage can be correctly disposed.â€‹
